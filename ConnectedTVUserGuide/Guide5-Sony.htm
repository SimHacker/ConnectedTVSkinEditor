Sony Clié Jog Dial Support
Clié Jog Dial Support ConnectedTV (version 1.19 and above) supports the Sony Clié Jog Dial, letting Clié users browse and tune TV shows without touching the screen, by just rotating and pressing the Jog Dial wheel.

The instructions are simple:

  • Roll the dial up and down to select choices within a page, and across pages.
  • Push in on the jog dial to tune the channel for the currently selected station or show.
 
Clié Jog Dial Back Button
Some Clié Jog Dial wheels have a 'Back' button alongside them. Pressing it does the following in these different contexts:
  • From Home page:
    Go back to your Palm's Applications page - exiting ConnectedTV;
  • From Show Details page:
    Go back to the ConnectedTV Shows page;
  • From other pages:
    Go back to ConnectedTV's Home page.
 
Clié High Resolution Display Control
If your Sony Clié supports high-resolution graphics and text, an additional button on the User Settings page will be visible, to let you disable or enable this feature for ConnectedTV.
